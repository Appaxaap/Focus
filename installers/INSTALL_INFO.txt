Focus for Windows — Beta Information

Thank you for testing Focus on Windows.

This is the first public beta release of Focus for desktop.
It is not yet production-ready.

Please be aware:
• Some features may be missing or unfinished
• Notifications and syncing are not fully implemented
• Performance and stability may vary between systems

Your data is stored locally on your device.
We recommend avoiding critical or long-term task data during beta testing.

Your feedback helps shape future releases.
